EngSoc Programs Director

December 2023 – November 2024

In 2024, I was successful in my application to become a Director in the UNSW Engineering Society's Programs Portfolio. The director team has full control of the vision and execution of EngSoc's programs, which include Applied Consulting for Engineers (ACE) and Peer Mentoring. We liase with our industry sponsors, host workshops, organise catering, conduct interviews and are responsible for the general smooth running of events. I am in charge of coordinating with the IT porfolio to set out the requirements for our Peer Mentoring website, and I oversee the database, hosted on firebase. This has given me valuable backend experience.

EngSoc Programs Subcommittee

February 2023 – November 2023

Before I was a Programs Director, I was part of the Programs Subcommitte. I contributed to all the same programs under the direction of the 2023 Programs directors. I presented workshops as part of the now defunct Engineering Upskilling Program (EUP), where I taught students soft skills such as presentation and teamwork. At the end of the year, I was in charge of hosting a charity day to raise money and awareness for Engineers Without Borders (EWB).
